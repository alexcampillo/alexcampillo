Tapeography:

Design, Motivational Posters

Tapeography was a series of motivational posters I created for Forum Coworking. The only materials used were electrical tape and white paper. Sometimes it feels good to just step away from the computer and try and make something you're proud of with whatever else you have. I ended up making a series for my apartment as well, they keep me going throughout the day.

CPUrx Rebranding:

Branding, Logo Design, Web Design

CPUrx, in business for 10 years with the same logo, same everything. This is my redesign of their website, logo and aesthetic. Just in time for their expansion to San Francisco!

Dessy's PopPopPop Up Party:

Design, Illustration, Event Poster

Dessy's PopPopPop Up Party is a monthly pop up event featuring a different themed talent show each time. This particular event featured a night of cliffhanger stories. Who is Dessy? Where will the next PopPopPop Up Party be? I'll never say!
