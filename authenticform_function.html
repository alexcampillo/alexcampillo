Project Highlights

Authentic Form & Function team, In this slideshow you will find some of my personal favorite projects I've worked on. While they may not be my best technical design work, I think they make up for in concept, execution and personality. I believe your design is only as good as the idea it supports, nobody cares what it looks like if what you say is dumb.

The Wang Thang

The Wang Thang was an on-demand chicken wing only restaurant I put together with my friend (teamwork yeah!). We we're up and running in one week! I doubled as head chef and designer and we double-teamed twitter. I drew so many chickens to get to this final concept poster.

In our first two weeks we gained quite a twitter following, even a celebrity endorsement from Derrick Carter, got press from DNA Chicago, Chicagoinno and Urban Daddy. We were also picked up by the food delivery service Caviar.

If you're walking around the west loop of Chicago be on the lookout, wang thang posters are still wheat pasted everywhere!

Mericastock

"I don't like this... but I LOVE these pictures!" my client exclaimed. A silhouette of an 18-wheeler flying down the desert highway with an american flag burned into the sunset was full-span across my screen. "We get so many compliments on our website's photography." That was it, the market had just been dangled in front of my face! So I took it.

Mericastock was born over a weekend out of this bizarre exchange with my client. I made a logo, built a website and Photoshopped a couple of American flags into some open source stock photos I found and Mericastock was in business!

Mericastock's newsletter has over 100 subcribers and counting! It was also featured in featured in Web Designer Depot's March 2015 New Resources List. Someone even bought a mug on it's Shopify webstore, my first big win as a product designer!
