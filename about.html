Who Is Alex Campillo?

some information

Alex Campillo is a designer, developer and entreprenuer from Chicago, Illinois. Since graduating from the University of Illinois at Chicago he has gone on to create and grow multiple startups, including AppDevy, which was named one of Chicago's 50 startups to watch this past year!

In his spare time Alex loves to meet dogs, make posters and fill the world with his side projects. Also known as 'Alex's Adventures'. Check out his latest one, Mericastock here!

Alex currently runs an independant studio out of his apartment in Chicago. He's always up for working on cool projects for good people. If you have a cool project whether it's design, development, or both and you're a good person feel free to reach out to Alex.
